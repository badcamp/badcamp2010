  • Drupal and Virtualmin: Managing Servers Without Excruciating Pain

    Drupal and Virtualmin
    Managing Servers Without Excruciating Pain

    Take your Drupal sites to the next level with the free, open-source Webmin control panel and its powerful Virtualmin plug-in. When used together, they are excellent tools for creating test and development sites, hosting client websites or even managing a full-blown online infrastructure for running a business.

    Similar to cPanel and WHM, respectively, Webmin and Virtualmin can manage dozens or even hundreds of domains and subdomains and make it easy to manage mail accounts, SSL certificates, Git and Subversion repositories, mailing lists, and more with a point-and-click web interface.

    Did we forget to mention, Webmin and Virtualmin are free* and open-source? Compared to site licensing costs of cPanel, WHM, Plesk and other proprietary control panels, Webmin and Virtualmin cost only as much as a basic $10-20/month VPS account that they are installed on.

    * While there is a commercially supported version of Virtualmin, this not a sales-oriented presentation and Virtualmin Pro will not necessarily be covered in this session. There is a new Virtualmin referral program, however, and any referrals from this session directly benefit the LA Drupal user group and not the presenters. ^
