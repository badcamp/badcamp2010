Hot or Not: Evaluating modules for fun and profit

Is this module any good? What module should I use for this feature? Will this module still be around in a year or has the community already moved on to the new hotness?

These are some of the many questions new Drupalers face when they first get started. More experienced folks have almost a sixth sense about such things because they've been involved in the development of half of them, but not everyone is so well-connected.

This session will offer guidance and recommendations for how to evaluate Drupal modules. It's not a fixed algorithm, but more of the heuristic indicators to look for to indicate how likely it is that a given module will be the best thing since sliced bread or the worst idea since the cast iron mattress.
